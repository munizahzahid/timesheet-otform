*TSSB Portal - Quick Guide*

*1. LOGIN*
- Open portal website
- Enter email & password → Login

*2. TIMESHEET*
- Menu → Timesheets
- Select month/year → Create
- Upload attendance PDF/Excel (optional)
- Fill in hours for admin job & project rows
- Click Save Timesheet

*3. OT FORM*
- Menu → OT Forms
- Select month/year & form type → Create
- Click Autofill from Attendance
- Fill in planned & actual OT hours
- Click Save OT Form

*4. SUBMIT*
- Open your timesheet/OT form
- Click Submit for Approval
- Type your name as signature → Confirm

*5. CHECK STATUS*
- Draft = still editing
- Pending = waiting for approval
- Approved = done!
- Rejected = fix & resubmit
